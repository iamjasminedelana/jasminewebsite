Policy.

How your information is collected, used, and protected on jasminedelana.com.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jasmine Delana (“we,” “us,” or “I”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit jasminedelana.com and related pages.

1. Information We Collect

  • Analytics data. With your consent, we use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors use the site — such as pages viewed, time on site, general location, and device or browser type.
  • Information you provide. When you submit a contact or inquiry form, we collect the details you enter (such as your name, email, and message) so we can respond. Forms are processed by Netlify.
  • Cookies. Small files stored in your browser that enable analytics and remember your cookie preference.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To understand and improve the site, content, and services.
  • To respond to inquiries and deliver requested services.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3. Cookies & Your Choices

On your first visit, a banner lets you Accept or Decline analytics cookies. Until you accept, analytics are disabled by default (Google Consent Mode). You can change your choice at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer, or by clearing cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics / Tag Manager — website analytics.
  • Netlify — website hosting and form processing.
  • 815collection.com — our apparel store runs on Squarespace with a print-on-demand fulfillment partner; purchases made there are governed by their policies.
  • Links to third-party websites are not covered by this policy.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (for example, the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under the CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to opt out of certain processing. To make a request, email hello@jasminedelana.com.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain inquiry information only as long as needed to respond and keep reasonable records, and we take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

7. Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent version.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email hello@jasminedelana.com.
